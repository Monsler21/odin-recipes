Description

This is a taco recipe.

  • Skirt Steak
  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Chilli Powder
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan
  2. Marinate steak by mixing with spices
  3. Add steak in hot oil over medium heat
  4. Stir steak until brown
  5. Assemble tacos
