Description

This is my grandmother's personal lasagna recipe.

  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Sauce

Steps

  1. Add sauce to bottom of casserole dish
  2. Add lasagna noodles to the top of the sauce
  3. Spread ricotta cheese on top of noodles
  4. Cover in meat sauce
  5. Repeat two more times
  6. Add mozzarella on top
  7. Bake at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 30 minutes
